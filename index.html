🌟 Welcome To Camp Nova

Camp Nova is a fun and creative summer camp designed for students who want to enjoy their holidays while learning exciting new skills. Here students can participate in games, coding sessions, dance, storytelling, sports, painting, and many more amazing activities.

Our goal is to make every child feel confident, creative, and happy.
